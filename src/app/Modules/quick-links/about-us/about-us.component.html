Our Story

Mentordex was born from the realization that countless parents hire teachers, tutors, coaches, and instructors for their children in a myriad of areas from musical instruments (like piano and guitar) to athletic coaching (like swim lessons and soccer tryout prep); or hire tutors for academic subjects (like math and foreign language) and instructors for areas such as voice, dance, art, and sewing. The list goes on and on! Parents can feel stuck in their search, limited in their options of potential mentors.

Finding someone is usually done via word-of-mouth and it can be hard to find the right fit.

Surely there’s a better way.

What if there was one website entirely dedicated to helping parents search and hire mentors, tutors, teachers, and coaches for face-to-face learning sessions? What if that website was so intuitive that it could help parents narrow down their search based on their desired criterion, like budget, schedule, credentials, and even ratings from other parents? These websites exist for vacation rentals, booking event entertainment, restaurants, handyman work, and for online freelancing. Why not a website dedicated to helping parents find the right mentors for their kids?

Let’s do this.

Similarly, there are people out there who have spent years learning and fine-tuning their skills who would relish the opportunity to earn an income teaching their knowledge and skills to others. But there isn’t an inexpensive, easy way of reaching a vast audience of parents/ potential employers. There are already many mentors, teachers, tutors, and coaches who do earn an income imparting their knowledge to others, but would appreciate an easier way to find more students to teach.

Now there is a better way.

At Mentordex, we want to simplify the lives of parents and mentors. Furthermore, we hope our website will help enrich the lives of children everywhere. Children need face-to-face learning where possible. The opportunity to explore interests, develop talents, and foster inherent potential is what parents everywhere want to give their children. We hope to help parents do just that.
