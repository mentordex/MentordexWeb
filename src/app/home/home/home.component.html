How It Works

It is fun to inspire kids to dream big dreams, explore new interests, & foster potential. MentorDex is all about helping parents & mentors do just that!

  • parents
  • mentors

Find your mentor

Create a free account and gain access to the MentorDex. Scroll through mentor profiles with rates, ratings, and credentials.

Book your session

Each mentor has a calendar of availability. You can easily book a session online, and/or message the mentor directly to plan details.

Attend your lesson

Accompany your child to the lesson (we always recommend a parent or caregiver stay for the duration). Pay your mentor and leave a review!

Get Started

Apply to Mentor

Submit an application and set up a video interview with our friendly staff.

Set up your profile

Market yourself to potential clients with your credentials, training, education, hourly rate, and more. Sync your calendar & list your availability, and watch your booking requests come in!

Get to work!

Confirm booking request and communicate with clients. Teach your lessons and earn your income. All on your terms!
